A flagship advocacy programme dedicated to promoting and protecting the rights of persons with disabilities. A nationwide awareness and advocacy campaign that challenges stigma, promotes accessibility, and fosters equal opportunities for persons with disabilities through public education, community engagement, and strategic partnerships.

4. GreenAbility

A sustainable development programme that empowers persons with disabilities through climate-smart agriculture, green entrepreneurship, environmental conservation, and livelihood opportunities that promote economic independence.

5. Discovery Award

A platform that identifies, recognizes, and promotes innovative grassroots solutions developed by and for persons with disabilities. The initiative showcases practical, affordable innovations that improve accessibility, independence, and inclusion across Africa.
